Roadmaps

1st semester

Start your BTech journey with positive attitude and motivation. Start exploring all the domains and do a complete research on it. Also learn one programming language that fits you well.

2nd semester

Learn all the theoretical concepts of your desired domain and practice coding problems daily. Maintain your college subjects side by side too. Make your linkedin account and post your achievements.

3rd semester

Make some mini projects in your chosen software field through our tutorials and stay updated about upcoming requirements. Showcase them in your resume.

4th semester

Start learning data structures and algorithms in your desired programming language and master it in this semester. Give coding contests too. Solve problem of the day on leetcode and gfg.

5th semester

Try to make some mini core projects(hardware) related to your engineering branch. Also continue your software domain and make pro projects. Gain all the skills with perfection.

6th semester

Polish your skills by doing online internships in your preferred profile and let your technical mind grow more. Add the internship experiences in your resume and your linkedin account.

7th semester

Now, it's time to start preparing for interviews. Polish all your skills and learn all core subjects like DBMS, OOPS, and analytical skills too.

8th semester

Companies come and hire brilliant students for offical internship and give PPO to those who performs well. Off campus placements are held in this semester. Stay positive and motivated. Remember, You are the best.
